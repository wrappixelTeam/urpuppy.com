The Affenpinscher is a toy breed that originated in Germany, with a history dating back to the 1600s. The name "Affenpinscher" means "Monkey Terrier" in German, reflecting their distinctive appearance and playful personality. They were initially bred to control pests in homes and stables, serving as effective ratters. Over time, they gained popularity as companion animals, particularly among nobles and the bourgeoisie.
